Invader - Reinvention of Vertical Shoot-em-up Game

  • Quote

Post by hengying » Thu Apr 14, 2011 1:00 pm

Almost everyone has played some kind of shoot-em-up game, but did you ever think about how the player’s plane could upgrade it’s weapon? This Invader game could tell you the secret.
Weapon towers floating in the Universe, if a power source attached it, it would become to an active weapon, shooting at opponents. If it lost the power source, it will be floating again.

LÖVE 0.7.1 is needed to play this game.

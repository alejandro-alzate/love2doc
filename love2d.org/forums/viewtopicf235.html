Post by Diehl » Sat Mar 15, 2014 1:31 am

Hello everybody!

After months of work, we finally released our first game!

"Zuli is a fun, but challenging arcade game where you take the role of a little albino bat who has to escape from the cave he lives in.

Avoid the deadly foes that fly around the cave and eat all the bugs to release Zuli's power (press Space).

Make sure that any of the enemies you encounter doesn't touch you or you'll have to start the level all over again. Have fun!"

Available languages​​: English and Portuguese.

We would love for everybody who play the Zuli, send your feedback about the game.

Do not forget to have fun while playing!




.love file: https://bitbucket.org/MinusOneGames/zul ... /ZULI.love

Note: We developed this game using the Löve 0.8.0.


I take this post to thank the entire community of Löve who helped us with all the problems we faced during the development of this game. Thank you!

The graphics are cute and I like the music.

Most of the gameplay just consists of luring enemies to open spaces so you can maneuver around them, which I didn't really enjoy.

There is no point in collecting the bugs on most levels, as you can get by the enemies without them.

I found the boss battle frustrating. You can only attack him after you collect all the bugs on the level, which is time consuming.
If a random rock hits you (which can happen quite easily) you have to start all over again, including collecting the bugs.
He also often spawns at random places off-screen and you have to go find him or wait for him to reappear near you.
I lost my patience before I could finish the boss battle.

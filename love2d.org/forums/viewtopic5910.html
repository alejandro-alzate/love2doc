Re: Probability of official Nintendo Switch port?

Post by MrFariator » Sat Dec 30, 2023 10:50 pm

Keep in mind that even if you have a game that you'd like to port to Switch (or other consoles), the people who have existing ports do not necessarily offer those ports for free. Some of them are running a business of porting love2d games to Switch (offering to port and publish games for a revenue split), or might like to have some compensation for offering their porting work to you (the deal you arrive to may vary). You might be able to get some tips or pointers on how to do the love2d port yourself, but for this you'd need to have signed the necessary NDAs with Nintendo.

However, as usual, the important thing to have first and foremost is a game to port. No use worrying about ports until that's covered.

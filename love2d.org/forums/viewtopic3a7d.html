I've been busy with RL the past month, so I threw together a quick animation in Love to warm back up. (Shameful, shameful code.)

Anyone who watched Toradora ought to recognize the idea.

Controls:
Spacebar to pause,
Enter to toggle fullscreen,
Mouseclick/wheel adds a new splash,
Up/right/plus to accelerate,
Down/left/minus to decelerate,
everything else exits.

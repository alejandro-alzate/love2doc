Why hello there. I've come here to post an actually finished project of mine (the first in a while) and I hope it will be as time consuming as I planned it to be.

Unfortunately, the UI is programmer art, and therefore butt ugly. The things that actually look good was stolen from this osu! beatmap. Thanks.

Okay, if you have seen Loituma/Leekspin you'll know just what this game is about.

If not, watch this youtube video (or at least half a minute of it):

So there you have the basic premise: A girl spinning a leek.
Now, the concept of this game is to spin that very leek as fast as you can.

You do this by clicking the super ugly "PLAY" button and then moving your mouse in circles as fast as humanly possible for 26 seconds.

My record is 283 spins per minute. How fast can you go?

Warp Run

  • Quote

Post by AaronWizard » Sun May 27, 2012 6:07 pm

Gentlemen, I present to you Warp Run.




Your goal is to reach the exit (forever). You'll need to collect keys to open all the doors and the monsters will make your life difficult.

WASD to move by default, mouse to aim and shoot.
Be sure to collect mystery boxes, which you open with Space by default.

You may download the executable (Windows, Mac, and .love file) from Bitbucket.

Repository: https://bitbucket.org/AaronWizard/warp-run

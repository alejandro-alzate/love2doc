Löve Logo Graphics

If you are looking to add an animated LÖVE-splashscreen to your game consider taking a look at the splashes library.  


Official LÖVE icon and logo graphics for use in splash, title, credits screens, website etc.

App icon Game (.love file) icon Logo

These graphics are officially used since LÖVE 0.10.1 (forum announcement).

Download zip from https://love2d.org/logos.zip.

You can download the icons individually by pressing the "Raw" link/button on GitHub:
